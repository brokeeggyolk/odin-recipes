The Perfect Beef Lasagna

Description

This hearty beef lasagna is the perfect meal for cold winter months. Easy to make, and easy to reheat, it's ideal for you to eat in one sitting or to share with the whole family!

Feel free to alter this recipe however you like, the true perfect lasagna is the one crafted for the individual tastebud.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Sauce

  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • 450g Minced Beef
  • 5 Hot Italian Sausages
  • Italian Herbs
  • 1 White Onion
  • 1 Carrot
  • Beef Broth

Cheese Layer

  • 500g Ricotta
  • Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Egg
